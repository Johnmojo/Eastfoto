Mission

Our goals and mission

Our mission - A better place for photographers

EastFoto is a platform for photographers and promoting photography interest. The idea was a place where members can have opportunities to engage or support the community through training, exhibition, and collaboration.

What do we do?

We organize various exhibitions, monthly competition, mentoring programs. We also have outings too!

How it works?

From time to time, we'll organize a meeting fortnightly. Members are not required to attend the meeting unless having something important to voice. Member can choose where the meeting/outings are held and when it happens.

Every picture tells a story

First of all, it is critical to understand the current situation. We're committed to make this platform more friendlier. Over the years, we've provided people with open, social way for user to interact. Undoubtly, EastFoto has provided opportunity for user to learn and grow their skills and passion.
